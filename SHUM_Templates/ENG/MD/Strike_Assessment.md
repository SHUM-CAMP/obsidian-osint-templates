# Battle Damage Assessment (BDA)

## 1. Title
***Specify the name of the analytical task, e.g., "Strike on ammunition depot by FPV drone", or "Fire on factory premises", etc. The title is determined by the assigned objective — to refute, confirm, or establish whether a strike was carried out.***

## 2. Object
***Specify what exactly was struck or damaged.***

## 3. Unit
***Specify the unit that carried out the strike.***

## 4. Location
***Specify the region, settlement, and WGS-84 coordinates in decimal format.***

## 5. Date and time
***Date and time of the strike.***

## 6. Means
***Specify the means by which the strike was conducted.***

## 7. Evidence
***Attach images or video and statements from official sources.***

## 8. Sources
***Provide a list of sources as hyperlinks or references.***

## 9. Assessment
***Formulate analytical conclusions.***

## 10. Additional notes
***May include supplementary information not requiring mention in the main body of the report: witness comments on social media, prior strike history on the same object, etc.***
