# Report on Organization: "___"

## Reporting guidelines

1. The report must be completed in a single language throughout.
2. Proper nouns, names, etc., must be given in the original language in quotes.
3. Visual presentation and readability must be self-formatted to minimize additional editing.
4. In addition to formal organizational data, factual information must be analyzed and stated.
5. A significant portion of the report consists of interpreted findings — data must not simply be listed but interpreted in context.
6. Do not duplicate information within the report (introductions and conclusions may overlap, but no more than twice); do not artificially inflate the report with excessive text or AI-generated filler.
7. Every assertion (except self-evident ones) must include a source, justification, or identifier supporting it.
8. Include important details absent from the template where relevant: ID card numbers, vehicle plates, etc.
9. For vacancies or personnel in technical specializations — specify software and hardware (applications, machinery, etc.) identified during analysis.
10. Information obtained via AI must be verified, analyzed, interpreted, or refuted.
