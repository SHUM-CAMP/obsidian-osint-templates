# TABLE OF CONTENTS

## Introduction

In the introduction, specify the purpose of the report (for example, a comprehensive analysis). Describe the enterprise, what it specializes in, and any other information that would be appropriate for the INTRODUCTION.

## 1. General Information About the Organization

In the general information section about the organization, it is necessary to indicate: the status of the enterprise, registration date, number of employees, TIN, OGRN, KPP, legal address, and contact details.

## 2. Structure, Management, Contacts

A section about the organization as a system. If data is available, indicate: the structure of the institution, governing bodies, contact information, and positions.

## 3. Main Areas of Activity

Indicate the main areas of activity according to OKVED and other available data.

## 4. Performance Indicators

If data is available, indicate: general organizational indicators, management performance indicators, financial and economic indicators, personnel indicators, and qualitative indicators.

## 5. Locations

It is necessary to indicate all known locations, including legal addresses, with images and videos.

## 6. Social Media and Media Presence

Indicate all known social media accounts of the organization. Indicate all appearances of the organization or its representatives in the media.

## 7. Individuals

A section about specific individuals, where it is necessary to indicate biographical information about the organization's employees. Photos of the entire management and engineering staff are mandatory.

## Conclusions

In this section, it is necessary to write a general conclusion for the report, briefly summarizing each section.

## Appendices

The APPENDICES section contains useful material that was not included in the report itself (for example, additional information, secondary information, screenshots, off-topic material, etc.). It is recommended to begin each appendix on a new page and label them with letters, for example: Appendix A, Appendix B. If an appendix is directly related to the main text, the main text should include a reference to the appendix, for example: "...contains 10 images (See Appendix A)."
