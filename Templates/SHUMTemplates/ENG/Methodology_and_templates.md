Successful prompts input (for GITHUB, website and tutorials)
Currently the requests really don’t matters only identifiers matter. For example if you input “please find the companies connected to Vladimir Putin” the tool will not literally answer this but will fill in one of the pre-selected templates (scan/search categories). 
Therefore, it is recommended that you be accurate as possible with your identifiers and chose the right scan/search for your needs. 
Here is the full option of scans/searches available: 
    • Full Investigation Report
        ◦ Provides a detailed report about the target entity with the following categories of information outputted: 
            ▪ Identity (Corporate information or biographical information)
            ▪ Connected persons
            ▪ Sanctions 
            ▪ Related entities and network
            ▪ Public Exposure and related media
            ▪ Risk Assessment 
            ▪ Conclusion
    • Sanctions Scan
        ◦ Scans through sanctions lists and data aggregators to determine if the inputted entity is under sanctions. 
    • Corporate Ties Scan
        ◦ Scans for any companies connected to this entity. 
    • Digital Footprint Scan
        ◦ 
    • Media Exposure Scan
        ◦ Scans specifically for public reporting related to the entity.
