# First 7 Days Onboarding

## Day 1
- Open `00_Home.md`, `01_QuickStart.md`, and `Operations/_index.md`.
- Review metadata contract.

## Day 2-3
- Complete one template-driven case draft.
- Link at least 3 source notes and 2 evidence notes.

## Day 4-5
- Run one QA cycle using `Reports/QA_checklist.md`.
- Submit one handoff note.

## Day 6-7
- Complete one methodology playbook exercise.
- Present findings with risk statement.
