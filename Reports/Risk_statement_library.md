# Risk Statement Library

Reusable risk sentence templates.

## Templates
- Low risk: "Current evidence indicates low immediate risk, with no confirmed escalation indicators."
- Medium risk: "Available evidence suggests moderate risk due to partially corroborated adverse indicators."
- High risk: "Corroborated indicators support a high-risk assessment requiring immediate review and mitigation."

## Usage rule
- Always reference at least one evidence note and one source note for each risk statement.
